Built on Experience, Driven by Purpose

For twenty years, I worked alongside my father in his custom cabinet shop. I learned what real craftsmanship looks like—the attention to detail, the pride in every joint, the satisfaction of delivering something built to last.

But I also saw the reality: truly custom cabinets had become a luxury most families couldn't afford. Meanwhile, big-box options left people settling for particle board boxes that fell apart in a few years. There had to be a better way.

That's why I started Wrightline. We bring two decades of custom cabinet experience to a model that actually makes sense for real people. No showroom overhead. No sales commissions. No markup on markup. Just solid construction, honest materials, and a straightforward process.

We keep everything simple—from this website to how we work with you. You tell us what you need, we design it together, and we build it right. No complexity, no confusion, just cabinets that fit your space and your budget.

Quality craftsmanship shouldn't be out of reach. And settling for junk shouldn't be your only option. We're here to prove there's a better way.
